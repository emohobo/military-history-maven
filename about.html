Meet The Historian

Nathan Grott, B.A. Military History, American Military University

Nathan Grott B.A. Military History
My name is Nathan Grott. I have been studying military history as a hobby for nearly my entire life, but officially made the jump to pursue this professionally in 2018. I enrolled at American Military University for a Bachelors in American Military History. I maintained exceptional academic studies throughout my program, placing on the Dean's List every semester for the duration of my college studies. After graduating Magna Cum Laude in February 2023, I took a year off to raise my newborn twin boys. As of Summer 2024 I have re-enrolled at American Military University to pursue my Masters in Military History, concentrating in Warfare since 1945. I am also enrolled in a certificate course for World War II studies. It is my passion to bring the study of military history to everyone in an approachable manner, while not sugarcoating any of the details. History is fierce, none more-so than Military History. My goal is to gather the stories of veterans and share them with the world, because every veteran's story deserves to be told, every one of their voices heard for their struggle in the name of their country.
